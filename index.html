Tutorial

Welcome to the Cosmere Editor! This tutorial will guide you through the features of the editor.

1. Start by writing your first few words in the editor.

2. Collect spren by maintaining a steady writing pace.

3. Unlock bonds with orders as you collect more spren.

4. Achieve daily writing goals to earn achievements.

5. Explore different themes and progress through the storyline.

6. Use the toolbar buttons to access various features such as downloading, importing, and viewing analytics.

7. Customize your experience in the settings panel.

Good luck and happy writing!
